Instructions:

1. Choose the size of the grid (10x10 or 30x30)
2. Enter the number of mines you want (10-30)
3. Enter the coordinates of a cell(x,y) to start the game
4. Enter the coordinates of a cell (x, y) and 'F' to place a flag on a mine
5. Enter the coordinates of a cell (x, y) and 'C' to clear up / check the adjacent cells
